Kyawthuite (2.16-2.19)

Partial Range Issues:
Rhodochrosite (1.597-1.817) - upper range exceeds 1.81
Bastnasite (1.717-1.818) - upper range exceeds 1.81
Hibonite (1.79-1.82) - upper range exceeds 1.81

Total: ~15-16 gem types cannot be fully tested on a standard refractometer. For these, gemologists must use alternative testing methods like specific gravity, spectroscopy, or professional lab equipment with extended RI ranges.
