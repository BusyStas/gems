Why Testing Matters

Proper gemstone testing is essential for:

  • Identification: Confirming gem species and variety
  • Treatment Detection: Revealing enhancements that affect value
  • Origin Determination: Establishing geographic source
  • Authenticity: Distinguishing natural from synthetic or simulant
  • Quality Assessment: Evaluating clarity and characteristics
