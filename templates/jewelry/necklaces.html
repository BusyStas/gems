Necklace Length Guide

The length of a necklace dramatically affects its appearance and how it complements your outfit and neckline.

  • Choker (14-16"): Sits high on the neck, perfect for V-necks
  • Princess (18"): Falls just below the collarbone, most versatile length
  • Matinee (20-24"): Falls at or above the bust, great for business attire
  • Opera (28-34"): Falls below the bust, elegant for formal occasions
  • Rope (over 34"): Can be worn long or doubled, very versatile

Styling Tips

Layer different lengths for a modern look, or choose a statement piece as your focal point. Consider your neckline and the occasion when selecting necklace style and length.
