Professional Assessment

Proper valuation of gemstones requires expertise and specialized equipment. Always seek professional assessment from certified gemologists when making significant purchases.

Important Certifications

  • GIA (Gemological Institute of America): The most recognized certification worldwide
  • AGS (American Gem Society): Known for strict grading standards
  • IGI (International Gemological Institute): Widely accepted globally
  • HRD (Hoge Raad voor Diamant): European diamond certification

Documentation & Authentication

Always insist on proper certification from reputable laboratories. Certificates should include detailed information about the gem's characteristics, any treatments, and high-quality photographs. This documentation is crucial for both insurance and future resale.
